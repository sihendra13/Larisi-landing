Section 03

Data We Collect

3.1 Account & Identity Data

  • Full name of the business owner
  • Business name and category
  • City or regency of business location
  • WhatsApp number (primary communication channel)
  • Email address (if provided)

3.2 Business Location Data

  • Geographic coordinates of the business location explicitly selected by the user on the dashboard map
  • Target radius configured by the user for each campaign

Important: Larisi does NOT track the real-time GPS location of users' devices. All location data is derived solely from explicit input provided by the user within the dashboard.

3.3 Creative Content

  • Photos and videos uploaded by the user for AI processing
  • AI-generated caption text and geo-stitched text overlays
  • User-edited versions of AI-generated content

3.4 Master Business Persona

  • AI-derived business profile based on analysis of uploaded content, including: detected business category, visual mood, communication tone, and audience segmentation preferences
  • This persona data is stored to improve the relevance of future AI generations for the same user

3.5 Third-Party Platform Data (via OAuth)

  • OAuth access tokens from Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok, and Google (YouTube) — provided voluntarily by the user through each platform's official authorization flow
  • Business account identifiers: Page IDs, Ad Account IDs, Instagram Business Account IDs
  • Campaign performance data returned by platforms after campaigns run: reach, impressions, engagement metrics, spend data

3.6 Usage & Technical Data

  • Activity logs: login events, campaign launches, AI generation usage count
  • Campaign analytics: reach, engagement, budget distribution across channels
  • Technical information: device type, browser type, access timestamps (for security and debugging purposes only)
Section 04

How We Use Your Data

  • To operate the AI Vision feature — analyzing uploaded content to build the user's master business persona
  • To generate Smart Geo Stitching text and per-platform captions based on the combination of business persona, location target, and audience segment
  • To operate the Geo-Radar Targeting system and compute real-time audience reach estimates
  • To publish content to the platforms selected by the user (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Meta Feed) on behalf of and with explicit authorization from the user's business account
  • To manage and optimize paid advertising campaigns (boost) as instructed by the user within the dashboard
  • To display campaign performance analytics within the Larisi dashboard
  • To contact users via WhatsApp for account activation, technical support, and relevant product updates
  • To improve the quality and accuracy of Larisi's AI models using aggregated, anonymized usage patterns — without identifying individual users
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations in the Republic of Indonesia

We do NOT use your data to: serve ads to you on behalf of third-party advertisers, sell or rent your data to any party, use your platform data (Meta, TikTok, YouTube) to target you on other platforms, or build advertising profiles unrelated to your own campaigns.

Section 07

Meta Platform — Specific Disclosures

This section provides specific disclosures required by Meta Platform Terms for applications using the Meta Marketing API and Instagram Graph API.

7.1 Data Obtained from Meta

  • Larisi accesses Meta data solely to provide advertising management services to the user who authorized access
  • Data obtained from Meta (including Page data, Ad Account data, Instagram account data, and campaign performance metrics) is used only for the purposes disclosed in this policy
  • Larisi does not use Meta Platform Data to make decisions that affect users' eligibility for credit, insurance, employment, or other similar purposes

7.2 Data Storage & Deletion

  • Meta Platform Data is stored only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law
  • OAuth access tokens are stored in encrypted form and are revocable by the user at any time through Meta's security settings
  • Upon account deletion, all Meta-derived data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Users can request immediate deletion of their data by contacting halo@larisi.id

7.3 User Consent Mechanism

  • Larisi obtains explicit consent from users before accessing any Meta account or data
  • Authorization is performed through Meta's official OAuth 2.0 flow — users are redirected to Meta's login page and explicitly grant permissions
  • Larisi displays the exact permissions being requested before the user approves
  • Users can revoke Larisi's access to their Meta account at any time via: Facebook Settings → Security & Login → Apps and Websites

7.4 Compliance with Meta Platform Terms

  • Larisi does not sell, license, or purchase Meta Platform Data
  • Larisi does not use Meta Platform Data to build targeting profiles for use outside Meta's platforms
  • Larisi does not transfer Meta Platform Data to data brokers
  • Larisi complies with Meta's Data Use Policy and Ads API Terms
  • Larisi does not scrape or harvest data from Meta outside of approved API endpoints

8.1 Data Obtained from TikTok

8.4 Compliance

  • Larisi does not use TikTok data for purposes beyond those explicitly stated in this policy
  • Larisi does not share TikTok-derived data with unauthorized third parties

Google / YouTube API — Specific Disclosures

Larisi uses YouTube API Services to enable users to publish video content to their YouTube channels. By using this feature, you also agree to be bound by the Google Privacy Policy.

9.1 Data Obtained from YouTube API

  • Larisi accesses YouTube API Services only to upload video content on behalf of authorized users to their own YouTube channels
  • Channel performance data (views, engagement) is retrieved only to display in the Larisi analytics dashboard for the authorizing user
  • Larisi does not collect, store, or analyze data of YouTube end-users (viewers) — only the channel owner's content and performance data

9.2 Quota & Rate Limiting

  • Larisi manages YouTube API quota responsibly and in accordance with YouTube API Services Terms of Service
  • API calls are made only in response to explicit user actions within the dashboard

9.3 User Control & Revocation

  • Users can revoke Larisi's access to their YouTube/Google account at any time via: Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access
  • Alternatively, users can revoke access directly at: https://security.google.com/settings/security/permissions
  • Upon revocation, Larisi will delete all stored YouTube OAuth tokens within 7 days

9.4 Compliance with YouTube API Terms

  • Larisi's use of YouTube API Services complies with the YouTube API Services Terms of Service
  • Larisi does not use YouTube API data for advertising targeting, data brokerage, or any purpose beyond publishing and analytics as described in this policy

Section 14

Your Rights

Under Indonesia's Personal Data Protection Law (UU No. 27/2022) and aligned with international standards, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data
  • Right to Restriction: Request that we limit the processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Revoke Platform Access: Revoke Larisi's access to Meta, TikTok, or YouTube accounts directly through each platform's settings at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at halo@larisi.id. We will respond within 14 business days. Identity verification may be required before processing certain requests.
